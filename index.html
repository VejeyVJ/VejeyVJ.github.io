M.Tech Thesis
Topology-Guided 2.5D Ensemble Fusion for Kidney Tumor Segmentation

Proposed a two-stage cascade architecture combining 3D kidney localization (nnU-Net) with an ensemble of three architecturally diverse 2.5D segmentation networks. Introduced SliceAttentionGate for adaptive inter-slice weighting, TumorAwareHead for explicit tumor encoding at the deepest representation level, and a topology-guided ensemble fusion strategy with evidential uncertainty calibration. Evaluated on the KiTS19 benchmark with 42 held-out validation cases. Paper co-authored with Ramya S.

Audio Forensics
Deepfake Audio Detection — SB-FAC

Designed a novel Sub-Band Frequency Attention Conformer architecture (~3.5M params) with sub-band decomposition across 4 frequency bands, parallel Conformer branches, and learned sigmoid-based frequency attention for interpretable spoofing detection. Deployed as a real-time Gradio web application with attention visualization.

Medical Imaging
CLAHE-Enhanced GI Disease Detection

Built an EfficientNetB0 + CBAM classification model with CLAHE preprocessing on LAB color space for 6-class endoscopic image classification. The contrast enhancement pipeline improved accuracy from 93.6% to 96.2% on Kvasir v2, with class-wise augmentation for handling pathological class imbalance.
